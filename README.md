<h1>Framely - Build Habits, Frame By Frame</h1>

<h2>Overview</h2>

<p>Framely is a habit tracker built to make people actually stick to their habits. On top of the usual features like multple habbits, visualizations, reminders, frequency settings, Framely offers a one-of-a-kind picture taking completion method. After a year of progress, you can look back at a special montage of 100s of photos of you going to the gym or taking a morning walk every day, which is a motivation like nothing else.</p>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
  <h4>Current Features</h4>
  <li>Unlimited Habits</li>
  <li>Color Coding</li>
  <li>Visualizations</li>
  <li>Unique Frequency Schedules</li>
  <li>Streaks</li>
  <li>Pauses & Covers for times when life gets in the way</li>
  <h4>Todo Features </h4>
  <li>Push Notification Reminders</li>
  <li>Photo Completion & Viewing</li>
  <li>Periodic Compilation Montages</li>
</ul>
